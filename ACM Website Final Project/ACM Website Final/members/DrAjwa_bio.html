Dr. Iyad Ajwa

Dr. Iyad Ajwa joined Ashland Unversity in 1997. He currently teaches many courses including: Java Programming, Mobile Applications, Linux Principles of Operating Systems, and various courses of Cyber Security and Cyber Ethics

He is currently the Chair of Mthematics and Computer Science Departments and has served on many other university and department committees. He has also served as the Faculty advisor for the AU student chapter of the ACM club for a number of years.

His education includes degrees from:
Kent State University, Ph.D. in Computer Science
Lehigh University, PA, M.S. in Computer Science
Lehigh University, PA, M.S. in Mathematics, and
University of Jordan, Amman, Jordan B.S. in Mathematics
